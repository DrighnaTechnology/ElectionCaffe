CURRENT STEP: Step 13 — Lock Election & Poll Day Preparation

Before poll day, go to **Elections** page → select your election → click **Lock Election**.

Locking freezes all election data to prevent accidental edits. Do this the evening before or morning of poll day.

Then print Voter Slips:
- Go to **Poll Day → Voter Slips**
- Configure slip options: show/hide photo, include QR code, show address, paper size (A4)
- Batch print booth-wise and distribute through booth committees before poll day

On poll day, use the **Poll Day** dashboard to:
- Monitor booth-level status in real time
- Track turnout as it comes in
- Coordinate with booth committees through the app

If corrections are needed after locking, a Tenant Admin can unlock the election.
