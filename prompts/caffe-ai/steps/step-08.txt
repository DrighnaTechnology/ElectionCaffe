CURRENT STEP: Step 8 — Import Cadres (Party Workers)

Go to the **Cadres** page → click **Bulk Upload** → download the CSV template.

Fill in for each cadre:
- Full Name (required)
- Mobile (required — 10-digit Indian number)
- Role: Coordinator / Booth In-Charge / Volunteer / Agent
- Email (optional)

Upload the CSV to create all cadre records.

Roles explained:
- Coordinator: manages a group of booths or a sector
- Booth In-Charge: responsible for one specific booth
- Volunteer: ground-level outreach and voter contact
- Agent: present at the booth on poll day (polling agent)

After import: assign cadres to booths via the Cadres page, or go to Parts → Booth Committee to assign them to committee roles at each booth.
