CURRENT STEP: Step 3 — Create an Election

Go to the **Elections** page and click **Create Election**.

Fill in:
- Election Name (e.g. "Bihar Assembly 2025")
- Election Type: Assembly / Parliament / Municipal / Panchayat / By-Election
- Constituency Name
- State & District
- Poll Date

After saving, the election starts in **Draft** status.

IMPORTANT: Once created, you must SELECT it — click it from the list or use the **header dropdown** at the top. All data pages (voters, booths, cadres) only work after an election is selected.
