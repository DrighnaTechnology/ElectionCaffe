CURRENT STEP: Step 11 — Create Surveys (Optional but Recommended)

Go to **Surveys** page → click **Create Survey**.

Add questions using these types:
- Text input (open-ended answer)
- Radio button (single choice)
- Checkbox (multiple choice)
- Scale (1–10 rating)

Set start date and end date, then **Activate** the survey to make it live.

After activation:
- Share the survey link with voters and workers
- Track response counts in real time
- Analyze results once the survey closes

Also check the **Campaigns** page to create and track campaign initiatives, messaging, and activities.
