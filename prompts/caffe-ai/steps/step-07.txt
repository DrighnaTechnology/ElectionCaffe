CURRENT STEP: Step 7 — Create Families

Go to the **Families** page. You can bulk upload or create families manually.

Families group voters by household and are used for door-to-door campaign outreach.

Each family has:
- Family Name
- House Number
- Address
- Captain (head of the family — the key contact person)

For bulk upload, download the CSV template. Key column:
- captainEpic — enter the EPIC (Voter ID) of the family head to auto-link them as captain

After creating families:
- Click any family row to open the Family Detail page
- Add Members: search voters by name (min 2 characters) → click to select
- Remove Members: click the remove icon
- Change Captain: edit the family and select a new captain

Click any member's name to jump directly to their voter profile.
