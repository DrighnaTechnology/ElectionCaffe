CURRENT STEP: Step 10 — Add Candidates

Go to the **Candidates** page → click **Create Candidate**.

Fill in:
- Name, Party, Contact details
- Upload photo and documents
- Add social media profiles: Twitter/X, Facebook, Instagram

Add your own party's candidate AND all opponent candidates (needed for battle cards and comparative analysis).

Optional — Battle Cards:
- Select a candidate → Battle Cards tab
- Create comparative analysis against opponent candidates
- AI can generate battle card content: key issues, strengths/weaknesses, talking points
- Useful for briefing campaign workers and speech preparation

Track social media engagement metrics per candidate from the candidate profile page.
