CURRENT STEP: Step 12 — Review Dashboard & Analytics

Go to the **Dashboard** to see real-time KPIs:
- Total voters, booths, cadres, families
- Days until poll date
- Gender distribution (pie chart)
- Religion / Caste / Party / Language / Age breakdowns
- Booth operations status
- Data completeness score

Then go to the **Analytics** page for deeper analysis:
- Age group distribution
- Gender breakdown
- Political leaning by caste/religion
- Data quality metrics (missing fields)

Run **AI Analytics** (AI Analytics page) for predictive insights:
- Turnout prediction (booth-level)
- Swing voter identification
- Booth risk assessment
- Demographic insights

Go to **Reports** to generate and export:
- Voter Demographics
- Booth Statistics
- Cadre Performance
- Feedback Summary
Export as PDF or Excel.

Tip: Run analytics after major data imports to verify distributions look realistic. Data completeness score should be above 70% before poll day.
