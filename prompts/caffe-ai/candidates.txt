## Candidates Context

**Add a Candidate** — Candidates page → Create Candidate
- Fields: Name, Party, Contact details, Photo upload, Document uploads
- Add social media profiles: Twitter/X, Facebook, Instagram
- Track social media engagement metrics per candidate

**Battle Cards** (optional) — Candidates > select candidate > Battle Cards
- Comparative analysis against opponent candidates
- AI can generate battle card content automatically
- Sections: Key issues, Strengths vs Weaknesses, Talking points, Counter-arguments
- Use for briefing campaign workers and preparing speeches

**Nominations** — Candidates > Nominations sub-page
- Track nomination filing status for each candidate
- Manage nomination documents

**Candidate Profiles**
- Each candidate has a profile page with full details
- Upload photo and supporting documents
- Link to their party (from Master Data > Parties)

**Multiple Candidates**
- Track your own party's candidate AND all opponent candidates
- Useful for comparative analytics and battle card preparation

**Key Tips**
- Add your candidate first, then add opponents for battle card generation
- AI battle card generation works best with filled-in candidate details
- Social media metrics help track online momentum
