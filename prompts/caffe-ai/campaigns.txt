## Campaigns, Surveys & Outreach Context

**Surveys** — create voter feedback surveys
- Surveys page → Create Survey
- Question types:
  - Text input (open-ended)
  - Radio button (single choice)
  - Checkbox (multiple choice)
  - Scale (1–10 rating)
- Set start date and end date
- Activate survey to go live
- Track response counts in real time
- Analyze results after survey closes

**Campaigns** — manage campaign initiatives
- Campaigns page → Create Campaign
- Track messaging, activities, events
- Monitor engagement metrics
- Link campaigns to specific elections

**News & Info** — monitor relevant news
- News page: fetch and display political news by category
- Categories: Local, State, National, Party, Opponent
- AI Analysis: analyze news for electoral impact, sentiment, recommendations
- Save analyzed news for team reference

**Actions** — task management
- Actions page: create and assign ground-level tasks
- AI can generate actions from news articles
- Track completion status across your team

**AI Tools** (credit-based) — Settings > AI Credits
- OCR for document scanning
- Text generation for campaign content (speeches, social media posts, press releases)
- Data analysis and predictions
- Credit balance shown in Settings
- Credits are consumed per use — check balance before bulk operations

**Key Tips**
- Activate surveys before sharing the link with voters/workers
- Use AI Tools to generate WhatsApp messages, slogans, and talking points
- Monitor news daily and use AI Analysis to understand electoral implications
- Create actions from news items to turn intelligence into ground operations
