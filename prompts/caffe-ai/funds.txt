## Fund Management Context (optional module — must be enabled in Organization Settings)

Fund Management tracks campaign finances: donations received, expenses incurred, account balances.

**Accounts** — create funding accounts
- Types: Bank Account, UPI, Cash
- Each account tracks inflows and outflows separately

**Donations** — record incoming donations
- Fields: Donor Name, PAN number, Amount, Payment Method, Date, Notes
- PAN is captured for compliance (Election Commission requirements)

**Expenses** — submit and track outgoing expenses
- Fields: Category, Vendor/Payee, Invoice number, Amount, Date, Notes
- Approval workflow: submitted → approved/rejected by admin
- Categories examples: Printing, Transport, Events, Advertising, Staff

**Transactions** — complete financial ledger
- View all inflows (donations) and outflows (expenses) in one timeline
- Filter by date range, account, category

**Fund Reports** — period-based financial reports
- Summary by account
- Category-wise expense breakdown
- Donor summary
- Export as PDF or Excel

**Enable Fund Management**
- Go to Settings > Organization Setup > Features
- Enable "Fund Management" for your tenant
- Once enabled, Funds section appears in sidebar

**Key Rules**
- Must enable Fund Management feature first (Organization Settings)
- Expenses need approval before they are counted as spent
- PAN capture on donations above threshold is important for EC compliance
