## Family Management Context

Families group voters by household. Used for targeted outreach and door-to-door campaigns.

**Bulk Import Families** — Families page → Bulk Upload → Download CSV template

CSV columns:
```
Family Name (required)
House Number
Address
Captain EPIC (captainEpic — voter ID of the head of family, auto-links captain)
```

- Captain is the head/decision-maker of the household
- `captainEpic` auto-links the captain by their Voter ID (EPIC) — must be an existing voter

**Manual Family Creation** — Families page → Create Family
- Fill: Family Name, House Number, Address
- Then add members and assign captain

**Family Detail Page** — click any family row:
- **Add Member**: search voters by name (minimum 2 characters) → click to select → confirm
- **Remove Member**: click remove icon next to member
- **Change Captain**: Edit family → select new captain from members
- Click any member name → jumps to their voter profile

**Family Captains** — Families > Family Captains sub-page
- View all captains across all families
- Captains are key contacts for household-level outreach

**Key Rules**
- Import voters first — family members must be existing voters
- Each family needs at least a captain for effective outreach
- A voter can only belong to one family
- Use captainEpic in CSV for fast bulk captain assignment
